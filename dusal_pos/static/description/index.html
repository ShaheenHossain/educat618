Point of Sale Dusal Extension

Easy and very useful

Features:

  • Change Odoo logo with your company logo on POS
  • Add barcode and your company logo to POS ticket (Optional)
  • Show quantity of available products on POS
  • Out of Stock Products Handling options on POS (Hide, Restrict to sell)
  • Hide option for discount button of POS Numpad
  • Hide option for price change button of POS Numpad
  • Print wisely categorized products on receipt (Optional)
  • Print total item count on receipt
